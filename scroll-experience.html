Built around a single scroll source of truth.

Lenis owns the scroll position. GSAP’s ticker drives Lenis’s requestAnimationFrame. ScrollTrigger listens to Lenis’s scroll event. The result: every animation lines up with the pixel the user actually feels under their wheel or trackpad.

No double animators racing each other. No 16-millisecond drift between the smooth-scroll position and where ScrollTrigger thinks the page is. The pinning math, the parallax offset, and the reveal triggers all read from exactly the same number.

When you remove the friction of contention you get headroom for the things that matter — typography that breathes, photography that drifts, transitions that feel like the next beat in a measure rather than a UI tick.
